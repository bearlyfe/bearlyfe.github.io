ABOUT US

vision and mission

1

VISION

Our vision is to increase mental wellness of young adults as they progress through the early stage of adulthood.

2

MISSION

Our mission is to help young adults bear with life issues and build stronger resilience by means of providing content whereby young adults learn to identify emerging mental illnesses and methods to cope with life stressors, as well as activities and experiences that help young adults develop resilience and methods to cope with stressors.

What is BearLyfe?

BearLyfe is a one-stop platform that helps young adults bear with life issues for the betterment of their mental health and wellbeing. We aim to revolutionise the way young adults cope with stress and issues in their lives.

On our innovative online platform, we provide service to emerging adults based on the principles of education and participation. 


Education
We present young adults with content that matter to them in the most engaging and creative ways. Through our quality content, we aim to use help young adults with issues including relationship, stress relief, and mental health.

Participation
We strive to help young adults achieve a balance in life through activities and experiences by working with hosts. Our hosts provide an array of activities ranging from arts and craft, health and wellbeing to sports and outings.
