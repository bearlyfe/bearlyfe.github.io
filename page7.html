CUTE ANIMAL POTTERY

ARTS AND CRAFTS

This activity will be held in the Creative.Studio. You will experience the creation of a pottery product with our eager guidance and assistance. We've prepared tools for you (including your own apron), and we look forward to providing you with a pleasant, hands-on experience. We will teach basic pottery knowledge, including the lore surrounding locally-produced pottery and how to design a pottery product. Then, you'll be able to attempt your own pottery work - cute pottery animals of your very own! We hope that you'll take more than your work away from this workshop. What we value the most is the process of pottery-making, and your presence in our concrete space.
