MENTAL ILLNESS IS A DISEASE

Psychiatry is a field in medicine that deals with mental illnesses. Just as our body falls ill, so does our mind. The changes in the chemicals of our brain that help us to function lose their balance. This causes a change in our mood and our cognition. There are a lot of different mental illnesses. Amongst them, the most common ones are anxiety disorders, depression, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia. These illnesses may manifest early in life and may affect all of us.

How do mental illnesses affect us? You might ask how these illnesses come about. Maybe somewhere along the line in the family tree there are relatives who have mental illnesses? Environmental factors are another reason why people develop mental illnesses. Often, it is the synergy of genetic factors and environmental factors that cause mental illnesses to develop.

There have been many misconceptions concerning mental illnesses in human history. People have been ignorant of its disease status until fairly recently. Now, it is known to us that mental illnesses are illnesses just like diseases of our body. It requires treatment and management by mental health professionals.
