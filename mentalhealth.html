MENTAL HEALTH

It is important to remember not to keep a mental illness hidden. Let people around you who care about you know about it. They may be able to provide support and help you through hard times. 

The mind is a fundamental part of the human body. We are creatures of “body” and “mind”. As it is such a significant part of our body, it is essential that we keep it healthy.

Psychiatry is a field in medicine that deals with mental illnesses. Just as our body falls ill, so does our mind.


People with mental illness feel that they are treated differently because of their illness. They feel that their disease is a sign of weakness and failure. As a result, people may be in denial of their disease and become more reluctant to seek help.

It is known to us that mental illnesses are illnesses just like diseases of our body. They require treatment and management by mental health professionals. Therefore, it is essential that we encourage those with mental illnesses to seek help.

It is common for each and every one of us to experience anxiety in our daily lives. Anxiety is the emotional and physical fear response to a perceived threat. However, not all of us who have anxiety suffer from anxiety disorder.

Stress can be part of positive experiences, such as going on a date, starting a new job, and helping us get the better of ourselves. However, too much stress can be crippling and detrimental to our mental health. 

Each of us go through hard times, but different people cope differently. Some people cope by harming themselves. Bear in mind self-harm is not a suicide attempt, but actually a way to cope with really painful feelings.

There is a need to act on our stress. We are dictated by our mental processes and the life events that cause them.

Mindfulness allows us to be aware of our own existence that sometimes we might forget.

The difference between humans and machines is that humans are much more versatile and that we have emotions. While it is obvious to most people that we are very different from machines, a lot of people tend to forget that we are not machines in that we need rest and to decompress.  

In the whole wide world, the second most common cause of death after accidents is suicide among young people. If you are thinking about committing suicide, or if you are worried about someone else who might have such thoughts, you should tell someone.

It is normal that moods fluctuate. For people with bipolar disorder, however, this fluctuation becomes extreme and out of proportion to the things that are going on in life.

Human beings have been social animals since the very beginning of time. This is how human beings have flourished and survived the challenges throughout the centuries.
